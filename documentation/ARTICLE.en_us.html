Iksir (ইকসির) is a pixel-perfect retro bitmap Bengali and Latin typeface crafted for digital nostalgia.

Rooted in the 8-bit and 16-bit video game aesthetics, Iksir brings rigid, blocky geometries to the complex curves and conjuncts of the Bengali script. Every glyph contour is hand-drawn, every conjunct pre-composed, and every edge strictly aliased to preserve pixel-grid integrity.

To contribute, see github.com/InanXR/Iksir.
